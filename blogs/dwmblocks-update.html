Important update for users of dwmblocks-royarg-git

07 Jul 2022

Due to a versioning mishap, an update to dwmblocks-royarg-git may display the following message from pacman: 

warning: dwmblocks-royarg-git: local (1.0.8f56741-1) is newer than royarg-repo (1.0.r75.8f56741c-1)

It is recommended to upgrade anyway by reinstalling the package, regardless of pacman complaining about a downgrade: 

$ sudo pacman -S dwmblocks-royarg-git
warning: downgrading package dwmblocks-royarg-git (1.0.8f56741-1 => 1.0.r75.8f56741c-1)
resolving dependencies...
looking for conflicting packages...
